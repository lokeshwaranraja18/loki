Questions

Course FAQs

Is the beginner course suitable for absolute beginners? +
Yes! The beginner course is designed for people who have never stitched before. We start from the very basics — understanding fabrics, using a needle and thread — all the way to completing a full garment.
How do I access the course after payment? +
Immediately after your payment is confirmed, you'll receive a login link on your registered WhatsApp/email. Click the link and you'll have instant access to all video lessons.
What materials do I need for the stitching courses? +
A basic sewing machine, fabric, measuring tape, scissors, needles, and thread. A complete materials list is shared before the course begins. Most items are available at any local fabric shop.
Is there a refund if I'm not satisfied? +
We offer a 7-day refund if you've watched less than 20% of the course content. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll process your refund within 3–5 business days.
Are the courses in Tamil or English? +
Our courses are taught in Tamil with English subtitles available. This makes them accessible to students across India.
